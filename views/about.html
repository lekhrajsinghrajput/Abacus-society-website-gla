ABACUS:

Abacus is a Computer Science Society in GLA University.This Soiety handles all events, workshops& Festivals in Computer Science Department. ABACUS organizes various creative events like inter-institute technical, co-curricular and extra co-curricular activities in the department. These events help students to improve their logical reasoning, communication skills and over-all personality.
